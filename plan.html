Feeling a bit lost? Fear not! This page is your go-to resource for optimizing your festival experience! Our meticulously curated collection of information is here to guide you through every step of the way as you plan your visit!

Apple Harvest Festival will host plenty of farmstand fresh apples and produce, delectable apple and seasonal baked goods, a variety of food trucks offering tasty bites, and a craft fair with artisans from around the region. There will also be live music and entertainment, plus a cider trail to enjoy refreshing cider in store and plenty of great apple and apples-inspired products for sale inside shops in and around Downtown.

Vendors

With over 100 vendors present at the festival, there are many opportunities to be introduced to the rich artisan and culinary community in Ithaca. Our food vendors feature apple-centric and other fall classics for you to taste, and the craft vendors offer homemade goods like soaps, jewelry, sculptures, clothing, and more! This is the perfect place to learn about and support Ithaca's small businesses that encompass the heart of the town.

Not Sure Where to Start? Here's What Festival Goers Have Said:

Emily, first-time festival goer and student

"I had an amazing time at the Ithaca Apple Harvest Festival! The atmosphere was electric, and there were so many activities to enjoy. My favorite memory was strolling through the vendor stands, sampling delicious apple treats, and chatting with local artisans. The cider donuts were out of this world! Can't wait to come back next year!"

John, festival enthusiast

"The Ithaca Apple Harvest Festival is a must-visit event! I've been attending for years, and each time, I discover something new and exciting. One of my favorite things to do is watch the live performances while savoring a freshly pressed apple cider. The vendor stands offer a variety of unique products, but my highlight is always picking up some homemade apple pie to enjoy later. It's a tradition I look forward to every autumn!"

Sarah, local resident

"I absolutely adore the Ithaca Apple Harvest Festival! It's the perfect opportunity to embrace the fall season and indulge in all things apple-related. My fondest memory is browsing through the vendor stands, admiring the beautiful handmade crafts and artwork. I found the most adorable apple-themed decorations for my home! The festival has such a warm and inviting atmosphere, making it a cherished experience for the whole family."
